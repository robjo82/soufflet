Button Accordion HN — FreePats, version 2024-03-29

These WAV files come from the Button Accordion HN sound bank:
https://freepats.zenvoid.org/Organ/accordion.html

They were recorded from a Hohner accordion by Jeff Stauffer in November
2023, contributed through Piotr Barz, and processed into a sound bank by
michael02022 for the FreePats project.

The files are dedicated to the public domain under CC0 1.0. The complete
license text is included in LICENSE.txt. Filenames were normalized to their
SFZ MIDI key centres for use in Soufflet; the audio content is unchanged.
